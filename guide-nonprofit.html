Nonprofit & Org Admin Guide

Set up your organization, create events, verify hours, and generate impact reports

Getting Started — Claim Your Organization

1. Create an Account

Sign up with your organization email (email/password or Google).

2. Search for Your Nonprofit

Go to "Nonprofit Search" → enter your organization name. We search the IRS nonprofit registry.

3. Claim Your Organization

Tap "Claim This Organization" → enter your name, role, and contact email. A verification email is sent.

4. Verify via Email

Click the "Verify Organization" link in the email (expires in 7 days). Your account is upgraded to Org Admin.

5. Access Your Dashboard

After verification, you land on the Org Admin Dashboard with full event and volunteer management.

Creating Events

1. Tap "Create Event"

From your dashboard, tap the + button or "Create Event".

2. Fill in Event Details

Title, description, category (Environment, Education, etc.), date/time, address (with autocomplete), and maximum volunteer slots.

3. Add Shifts (Optional)

For multi-shift events, add up to 10 shifts with different time slots and volunteer caps.

4. Publish

The event goes live immediately. Nearby students will see it on their Explore tab. A check-in PIN is auto-generated.

Check-In PIN: Share this 4-digit code with volunteers who can't use GPS check-in. Find it on the event detail page.

Managing Registrations

When students register for your event, you can:

  • View all registrations with status (Confirmed, Waitlisted, Checked In, Verified)
  • See how many spots are filled vs. available
  • Post announcements to all registered volunteers
  • Cancel the event (all registered volunteers are notified automatically)

Verifying Hours

Automatic Verification (GPS)

If a student checked in via GPS, their hours are auto-verified on checkout. No action needed from you. The student sees "Auto-Verified via GPS" and receives a certificate immediately.

Manual Verification (PIN/QR)

1. Check Pending Verifications

Dashboard → "Pending Verifications" shows all hours awaiting your approval.

2. Review Evidence

Click the evidence icon to see e-signatures and photos the student submitted.

3. Approve or Reject

Click "Approve" to verify hours or "Reject" if incorrect. Students are notified either way.

4. Bulk Approve

Select multiple pending verifications and approve them all at once.

Self-Reported Hours

Students can self-report hours for work done outside ImpactBadge. As the supervisor, you'll receive an email with a "Verify Hours" button. Click to approve or reject — no ImpactBadge account needed.

Impact Reports (Grant-Ready PDF)

1. Go to Analytics → "Download Impact Report"

Select date range (defaults to last 6 months). Optionally include top 20 volunteers.

2. Generate Report

ImpactBadge compiles: total hours, unique volunteers, events hosted, repeat rate, check-in rate, category breakdown, monthly trends, and estimated community impact.

3. Receive PDF

A professional grant-ready PDF is emailed to your account and available for download. Includes your EIN and organization details.

Grant Season Tip: Generate reports in Q4 (October-December) and Q1 (January-March) when most grant applications are due. The report provides all the data foundations typically require.

Analytics Dashboard

Your org dashboard shows at a glance:

  • Total Events — events you've hosted
  • Unique Volunteers — individual students who attended
  • Verified Hours — total hours across all events
  • Check-in Rate — registrations that showed up
  • Hours by Category — which categories your events serve
  • Monthly Trends — volunteer activity over time

CSV Import & Webhooks

CSV Hours Import

Upload a CSV file with columns: Student Email, Event Title, Date, Hours, Description. Up to 500 rows per upload. Students not yet on ImpactBadge are saved as pending — their hours are applied when they sign up.

Webhook Integration

Connect your existing volunteer management system to push hours automatically:

  1. Go to Settings → "Generate Webhook Key"
  2. POST to https://us-central1-impacthub-prod.cloudfunctions.net/webhookListener
  3. Include your API key and an array of events (student email, title, date, hours)
  4. Rate limited to 10 requests/hour, 1,000 events per request

Web Portal

Access your full dashboard from any browser at impacthub-prod.web.app. Log in with your org admin credentials. The web portal includes:

  • Dashboard with stats and pending verifications
  • Analytics with charts
  • Volunteer CRM with search and detail view
  • Availability heatmap
  • CSV import tool
