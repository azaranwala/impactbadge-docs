5. Honest Assessment — Where You Are Today

Current Valuation: $250K-$500K (code + IP value only)
Without users, revenue, or a signed district contract, the valuation is based purely on the codebase, feature set, and Clever integration readiness. No acquirer will pay a premium for a product without traction, regardless of how feature-rich it is.
What changes everything: TRACTION
The single most important thing to increase acquisition value is getting real students using the app at real schools. One signed HCPS pilot with 500+ active students changes the conversation from "interesting technology" to "proven product." This is the difference between $500K and $3M+.

7. Most Likely Acquisition Scenario

Scenario: Kahoot/Clever acquires ImpactBadge (30% probability, 12-24 months)

Why: ImpactBadge is already a Clever Complete partner with SSO + Rostering + LMS Connect built. Kahoot (Clever's parent) loves gamification — it's their core DNA. ImpactBadge adds student engagement (XP, badges, streaks) to Clever's infrastructure play.

How it happens: ImpactBadge gets Clever certified → listed in Clever App Gallery → gains traction at 20+ districts → Clever's partnership team notices growth → acquisition conversation starts.

Price range: $3M-$10M depending on traction at time of offer.

What they really buy: The gamification engine + parent dashboard + Common App export. These are features Clever doesn't have and can't easily build (they're an infrastructure company, not a consumer app company).

8. What NOT to Do

Don't optimize for acquisition — optimize for traction.
  • Don't pitch acquirers before you have 5,000+ active students — you'll get lowballed
  • Don't take a $500K acqui-hire offer when 6 more months of growth could 5x the price
  • Don't add features for an acquirer's roadmap — add features that get you more students
  • Don't spend time on investor decks — spend it on signing HCPS and DCPS
  • The best acquisition outcomes happen when the founder isn't trying to sell
