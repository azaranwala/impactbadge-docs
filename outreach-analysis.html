Key Insight: 60-70% of schools still use paper forms and spreadsheets. x2VOL is the biggest competitor but charges per-school fees. Helper Helper has Clever + ClassLink but no gamification. ImpactBadge's free model + gamification + Common App export is the differentiation play.

4. Strategic Recommendations

Highest ROI Targets (respond first, convert fastest):
  1. HCPS (85%) — your pilot district, building for their CSL, warm relationship
  2. DCPS (80%) — brand new 100-hr mandate starting THIS YEAR, paper-only, desperate for tools
  3. Deep Run HS (75%) — single school, fast decision, active Key Club, HCPS pilot candidate
  4. Arkansas DOE (70%) — state-level contact, new mandate, 480K students with one meeting
  5. Richmond SPCA (70%) — direct contact (Amber Stevens), no existing tool, teens love animals
Avoid (for now):
  • LAUSD (35%) — massive bureaucracy, 18+ month procurement cycles, no warm intro
  • Districts already using x2VOL — switching cost is high; target districts NOT yet committed to a tool
  • HandsOn Greater Richmond (35%) — runs CFengage (competing platform), unlikely to adopt ImpactBadge
