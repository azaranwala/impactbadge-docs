Parent & Guardian Guide

Monitor your child's volunteer activity, set permissions, and stay informed

Linking Your Account to Your Child

1. Your Child Generates a Link Code

In the ImpactBadge app, your child goes to Profile → "Link Parent" → a 6-digit code is generated (e.g., A3F7K2).

2. Download ImpactBadge

Download the ImpactBadge app from the App Store (iOS) or get the link from your child.

3. Create a Parent Account

Sign up with your email. Your account is automatically set to the "Parent" role.

4. Enter the Link Code

On the Parent Dashboard, enter the 6-digit code your child shared. Tap "Link."

5. Connected!

You can now see your child's volunteer activity in real-time.

Multiple Children: You can link multiple children to your account. Each child generates their own 6-digit code.

COPPA Compliance (Under 13)

If your child is under 13, ImpactBadge requires your consent before they can use the app:

1. Your Child Signs Up

During signup, if they enter a date of birth making them under 13, they're prompted for a parent email.

2. You Receive a Consent Email

An email from ImpactBadge asks you to approve or deny your child's account. You can review what data is collected.

3. Approve or Deny

Click "Approve" to activate their account, or "Deny" to prevent access.

4. Account Activated

Once approved, your child can use ImpactBadge. Until then, they see a "Pending Consent" screen.

Your Rights Under COPPA

  • You can review what personal information ImpactBadge collects about your child
  • You can request deletion of your child's data at any time (email privacy@impactbadge.app)
  • You can revoke consent by unlinking your account
  • ImpactBadge does not share children's data with third parties for marketing

CSL Parent Consent Form (HCPS)

If your child attends Henrico County Public Schools, they need a CSL (Community Service Learning) parent consent form before logging volunteer hours. Your child can submit this digitally in the app:

  1. Student opens Hours tab → taps "CSL Parent Form Required" banner
  2. Reviews the 6 CSL guidelines
  3. Enters your (parent's) full name
  4. Toggles the consent agreement
  5. Submits — the digital consent is recorded

Unlinking Your Account

You can unlink from your child's account at any time from the Parent Dashboard. This removes your access to their data but does not affect their ImpactBadge account or hours.
