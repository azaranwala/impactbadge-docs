Onboarding Checklist

Your 15-Minute Setup Guide

1

Create Your Admin Account

Download ImpactBadge from the App Store. Sign up with your organization email. Select "Organization Admin" as your role.

Time: 2 minutes

2

Claim Your Nonprofit

Search for your organization by name in the nonprofit directory. Click "Claim This Organization" and enter your EIN for verification.

Time: 2 minutes

3

Create Your First Event

Tap "Create Event" from your dashboard. Add title, date/time, location (address auto-completes), max volunteers, and category. A check-in PIN is auto-generated.

Time: 3 minutes

4

Share With Your Network

Your event is immediately visible to nearby students on the map. Optionally share the event link on your social media or email list to boost sign-ups.

Time: 2 minutes

5

Event Day: Just Show Up

Volunteers check in via GPS when they arrive (auto-verified) or you share the PIN for indoor events. Hours calculate automatically on checkout. You'll see everything on your dashboard.

Time: 0 minutes of admin work

FAQ

Common Questions

Is it really free?

Yes. 100% free for nonprofits and volunteers, forever. Our revenue comes from school district subscriptions for compliance features. Your org admin tools have zero cost.

Do students need to download an app?

Yes. ImpactBadge is available on the App Store (iOS) now, with Android launching soon. Students sign up in under 2 minutes.

What if volunteers don't have the app?

You can share the PIN code displayed on your dashboard. Volunteers can also self-report hours afterward with photo evidence and e-signatures for your review.

How does GPS auto-verification work?

When a volunteer checks in via GPS within 300m of your event location, and checks out, their hours are automatically verified. No manual approval step needed from your side.

Can I import our existing volunteer data?

Yes. Upload a CSV with your existing volunteer records. We also support webhook integration if you use another VMS like VolunteerHub.

Can multiple staff manage our events?

Currently one org admin per organization. Multi-admin support with role permissions is on our roadmap and coming soon.

Is student data safe?

Absolutely. ImpactBadge is COPPA and FERPA compliant. Student data is encrypted, never sold, and parents have full visibility and control over their child's account.

Can we use this for adult volunteers too?

ImpactBadge is optimized for K-12 students, but adults can create accounts and check in to events. The gamification features (XP, badges) work for all ages.
