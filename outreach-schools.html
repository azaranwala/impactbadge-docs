Email Template for Districts with Mandates

Subject: Free tool to track [DISTRICT]'s [X]-hour graduation requirement — ImpactBadge

Hi [NAME],

I'm Mustufa, founder of ImpactBadge — a free app that automates student volunteer hour tracking for graduation requirements.

I'm reaching out because [DISTRICT] requires [X] hours of community service for graduation, and I know tracking compliance across [NUMBER] students is a significant workload for counselors.

ImpactBadge replaces spreadsheets with:
• GPS check-in at volunteer sites (no paper sign-in sheets)
• Real-time compliance dashboard — see which students are on track, at risk, or behind
• Monthly email digest to counselors: "12 students at risk, 3 below minimum"
• Automatic Naviance + Common App export for college applications
• Clever SSO + ClassLink integration — students sign in with school credentials

We're currently piloting with Henrico County Public Schools (VA) for their 80-hour CSL diploma seal.

Would you have 15 minutes for a quick demo? I can show you the counselor dashboard live.

Best,
