Student Guide

Everything you need to get started with ImpactBadge

Getting Started

Step 1: Create Your Account

You have 4 ways to sign up:

MethodBest ForWhat Happens
Email & PasswordAny studentEnter name, email, school, grade, Student ID → account created immediately
Sign in with GoogleGmail or Google Workspace usersOne-tap sign in → school auto-detected if using school email domain
Sign in with AppleiPhone usersFace ID / Touch ID → account created with Apple ID email
Sign in with CleverSchools using CleverClick from Clever portal → school info auto-populated from district SIS
Tip: If you signed up with Google or Apple, go to Profile → "Edit School Info" to add your school name, district, Student ID, and graduation year. This is needed for graduation hour tracking.

Step 2: Choose Your Interests

After signing up, pick the volunteer categories you care about — Environment, Education, Health, Animals, Hunger, Arts, Seniors, Community. ImpactBadge uses these to recommend events near you.

Finding & Registering for Events

1. Browse Events on the Explore Tab

See nearby volunteer events sorted by distance. Filter by category (Environment, Health, etc.) or switch to Map View.

2. Check Event Details

Tap any event card to see: description, date/time, address (tap for directions), available spots, and reviews from other volunteers.

3. Register

Tap "Register" to secure your spot. If the event is full, you'll be added to the waitlist. You'll get a push notification 24 hours before the event.

4. Multi-Shift Events

Some events have multiple shifts (e.g., Morning 9-12, Afternoon 1-4). Choose the shift that works for you.

Checking In & Logging Hours

1. Arrive at the Event

Open ImpactBadge when you arrive at the volunteer location.

2. Check In (3 methods)

GPS Check-In: Automatic if you're within 300 meters — just tap "Check In" and GPS verifies your location.
PIN Check-In: Enter the 4-digit code provided by the event organizer.
QR Code: Scan the QR code displayed at the event.

3. Volunteer!

The timer starts when you check in. Do your volunteer work.

4. Check Out

When you're done, tap "Check Out." Your hours are calculated automatically.

5. Hours Verified

GPS check-in: Hours are auto-verified immediately! You'll see "Auto-Verified via GPS."
PIN/QR check-in: Hours go to "Pending" — the org admin will verify them.

Tip: After check-out, you'll receive an email with a PDF certificate of your verified hours.

Logging External Hours (Self-Report)

Volunteered somewhere that doesn't use ImpactBadge? You can self-report those hours.

1. Go to Hours Tab → "Log External Hours"

Fill in: Organization name, activity description (20+ characters), hours, date.

2. Add Supervisor Info

Enter your supervisor's name and email. They'll receive a verification email.

3. Sign & Submit

Draw your e-signature and optionally add up to 3 photos as evidence. Tap Submit.

4. Supervisor Verifies

Your supervisor clicks "Verify Hours" in the email they receive. Once verified, the hours are added to your total.

Gamification — XP, Levels, Badges & Challenges

⭐

XP Points

Earn 100 XP per verified hour. Bonus XP for challenges, referrals, and streaks.

📈

Level Up

Progress through 7 levels: Greenhorn → Volunteer → Helper → Hero → Champion → Legend → Sage

🏅

Badges

Earn 11 badges: First Step, 5hr Hero, 10hr Champ, streak badges, and more.

🔥

Streaks

Volunteer on consecutive days to build streaks. Hit 100 days for the Century Streak badge!

🏆

Challenges

Join time-limited challenges like "Log 10 hours this week" for bonus XP rewards.

🏫

School Challenges

Compete school-vs-school! Your hours count toward your school's total on the leaderboard.

Leaderboard

Check your ranking on 3 leaderboards:

  • Global — All ImpactBadge students
  • School — Students at your school
  • Friends — People you follow

Graduation Hour Tracking

If your school has set graduation hour requirements, you'll see a progress bar on your Profile:

  • Green — On track (≥100% of expected hours by now)
  • Yellow — At risk (50-99%)
  • Red — Below minimum (<50%)

You'll also receive monthly push notifications if you're behind on hours.

CSL (Community Service Learning) — HCPS Students

If you're in Henrico County Public Schools:

  1. Submit the CSL Parent Consent Form (Hours tab → banner at top)
  2. Request Pre-Approval before starting at a new organization
  3. Track your progress toward the 80-hour Diploma Seal
  4. Seniors: submit all hours by April 30

Parent Linking

Your parent/guardian can monitor your volunteer activity:

  1. Go to Profile → scroll to "Link Parent"
  2. A 6-digit code is generated
  3. Share the code with your parent
  4. They enter it in their ImpactBadge app → accounts are linked
  5. Parent can see your hours, events, badges, and streak

Email Verification

You'll see a "Verify Your Email" banner on your Profile. This is optional but recommended — it protects your account and enables password recovery. Tap "Verify" and check your email for the link.
